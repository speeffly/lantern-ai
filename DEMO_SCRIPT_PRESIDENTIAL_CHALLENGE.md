## **Live Demo - Student Journey (3 minutes)**

### **Step 1: Assessment (45 seconds)**
*"Let me show you how Sarah, an 11th grader in rural Ohio, discovers her career path."*

**Actions**:
1. Navigate to http://localhost:3001
2. Click "Get Started" 
3. Complete 2-3 assessment questions quickly
4. Show different question types (interests, skills, preferences)
5. Enter ZIP code: "43001"

**Narration**: *"Our 12-question assessment uses AI to understand student interests, skills, and goals. Notice how we ask about both academic interests and hands-on preferences."*

### **Step 2: AI-Powered Results (2 minutes 15 seconds)**
*"Now watch as our AI generates comprehensive, personalized recommendations."*

#### **Tab 1: Career Matches (30 seconds)**
**Show**: Career matches with percentages
**Narration**: *"Our AI matched Sarah with healthcare careers based on her interest in helping people. Notice the detailed reasoning - not just a score, but why each career fits her profile."*

#### **Tab 2: Course Plan (45 seconds)**
**Show**: Year-by-year course recommendations
**Narration**: *"Here's where AI really shines - specific courses Sarah should take this year and next year. Biology and Chemistry for healthcare preparation, mapped directly to her career goals. This answers the question: 'What classes should I take?'"*

#### **Tab 3: Local Jobs (45 seconds)**
**Show**: Job opportunities within 40 miles
**Narration**: *"These are real job opportunities within 40 miles of Sarah's location. Notice the salary ranges, requirements, and distance. This shows students that good careers exist in their area."*

#### **Tab 4: Action Plan (15 seconds)**
**Show**: AI-generated pathway
**Narration**: *"Finally, our AI creates a step-by-step pathway from where Sarah is now to her career goal. Short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals with specific actions."*
